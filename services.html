What we compose.

Six core capabilities, fused into a single craft. Each service can be commissioned independently or composed into a full product lifecycle—from signal to global launch.

/ 01 · Programmatic

Programmatic Audio Synthesis

We design audio engines that generate, evolve, and respond in real time. Rather than shipping fixed sound assets, we compose rule-based systems that produce infinite, organic variation—giving every user a unique sonic experience without bloating app size or bandwidth.

  • Procedural sound design — generative engines for ambient, UI, and adaptive music layers.
  • Reactive audio — sound that responds to touch, motion, and contextual state in real time.
  • Lightweight runtime — no shipped samples; everything synthesized from compact rule sets.
  • Cross-platform — iOS, Android, and web implementations sharing a common rule format.

/ 02 · Interaction

Immersive Visual Interaction

We craft interaction grammar—gesture, motion, transition, response—that translates the cold logic of a system into something humanly felt. Every animation carries intention; every transition preserves continuity; every state communicates without words.

  • Motion systems — choreographed transitions, micro-interactions, and ambient motion.
  • Gesture languages — custom gesture vocabularies tailored to each product's domain.
  • Accessibility-first — reduced-motion, high-contrast, and assistive-tech parity.
  • Cross-device fluency — interaction coherence from phone to tablet to desktop.

/ 03 · Privacy

Privacy Engineering

Privacy is not a checkbox; it is a structural decision. We architect products so that the data simply does not need to leave the device. Where cloud is required, we minimize, encrypt, and disclose. The result is software users trust because there is, structurally, less to trust.

  • Local-first storage — by default, user data lives on-device, encrypted at rest with AES-256.
  • Zero-knowledge sync — when sync is required, end-to-end encryption prevents server access.
  • Consent architecture — granular, revocable, and clearly explained in plain language.
  • Global compliance — GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD, PIPEDA, DPDP, and PDPA-S.
Audit Your Privacy Posture
/ 04 · Native

Native iOS & Android Development

We build production-grade mobile applications for Apple App Store and Google Play—from concept through global launch. Every product is engineered native, optimized for the platform, and shipped with full compliance documentation for IAA, IAP, subscriptions, and regional regulations.

  • Swift & SwiftUI — modern iOS implementation following Apple's Human Interface Guidelines.
  • Kotlin & Jetpack — Android implementations optimized for Material Design 3 and Android 15.
  • Ad stack integration — AdMob, AppLovin MAX, Unity LevelPlay, Meta, Pangle, Vungle, ironSource, InMobi, Mintegral.
  • IAP & subscriptions — StoreKit 2, Google Play Billing v6+, transparent subscription UI.
  • App Store compliance — privacy labels, ATT, DSA, and review-ready submission packages.

/ 05 · AI

AI Generative Capabilities

When applications include AI-generated text, audio, image, or scene content, we engineer the experience with explicit consent, transparent labeling, and human-in-the-loop review. We follow EU AI Act and US state-level AI transparency requirements—every AI feature is auditable and explainable.

  • Content labeling — every AI-generated asset is clearly marked as such, distinct from human-created content.
  • Dual moderation — automated filters plus human review for borderline or sensitive outputs.
  • Responsible training — no user personal data is used to train or fine-tune models.
  • Disclosure — model identity, training data category, and known limitations are surfaced in-app.

/ 06 · Compliance

Global Compliance Engineering

In 2026, launching a global application means satisfying a thicket of overlapping regimes—DSA, GDPR, CPRA, VCDPA, LGPD, PIPEDA, DPDP, PDPA-S, and Apple/Google policies. We build compliance into the engineering process, not as an afterthought, so that audits, store reviews, and regulator inquiries are routine rather than disruptive.

  • Privacy policy drafting — region-aware, multilingual, kept current with regulatory changes.
  • App Store & Play submission — privacy labels, data safety forms, ATT and Privacy Sandbox alignment.
  • DSA transparency — ad repository, recommendation disclosure, content moderation framework.
  • Anti-fraud enforcement — IAA and IAP fraud detection, escalation policy, audit trails.
  • DSR handling — intake, verification, fulfillment within statutory windows.
